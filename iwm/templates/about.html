About I Want More

We're passionate about creating extraordinary shopping experiences that inspire and delight our customers. At I Want More, we believe that quality, affordability, and style should never be compromised.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, I Want More began with a simple idea: to create a shopping platform that offers premium products at accessible prices. Our founders, Emily and Michael, saw a gap in the market for a curated shopping experience that focuses on quality and customer satisfaction.

What started as a small online store with just 50 products has grown into a global marketplace with thousands of carefully selected items. Through perseverance, dedication to quality, and a customer-first approach, we've built a community of over 100,000 satisfied shoppers worldwide.

Today, we continue to expand our offerings while staying true to our core values of quality, affordability, and exceptional service.

Our Mission

At I Want More, our mission is to transform the online shopping experience by offering curated, high-quality products that enhance our customers' lives. We believe that everyone deserves access to beautiful, well-crafted items without breaking the bank.

We're committed to responsible business practices, working with ethical suppliers, reducing our environmental impact, and giving back to communities through our charitable initiatives.

By prioritizing exceptional customer service, transparent business practices, and continuous innovation, we aim to be more than just an e-commerce platform—we strive to be a trusted partner in our customers' journey to discover products they'll love for years to come.

Our Values

Quality First

We meticulously curate every product in our collection, ensuring each item meets our high standards for craftsmanship, materials, and durability.

Customer Satisfaction

Your happiness is our priority. We go above and beyond to ensure a seamless shopping experience, from browsing to delivery and beyond.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental footprint through eco-friendly packaging, responsible sourcing, and ethical business practices.

Community

We believe in giving back to the communities that support us, through charitable initiatives and supporting local artisans and businesses.
