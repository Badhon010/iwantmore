Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions about our products, services, shipping, and more.

Orders

How do I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing our shop, selecting the products you wish to purchase, and adding them to your cart. Once you're ready to checkout, follow these steps:

  1. Click on the cart icon at the top of the page
  2. Review your items and click "Proceed to Checkout"
  3. Enter your shipping and payment information
  4. Review your order and click "Place Order"

You'll receive an order confirmation by email once your order is placed.

Orders

How can I check the status of my order?

You can check the status of your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section. There, you'll find information about your order status, including whether it has been processed, shipped, or delivered.

You can also contact our customer service team at iwantmore.bd999@gmail.com or call us at 01952886888 with your order number for assistance.

Orders

Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?

We process orders quickly to ensure fast delivery. If you need to modify or cancel your order:

  • For orders that have not yet been processed: Contact us immediately at iwantmore.bd999@gmail.com or call 01952886888 with your order number.
  • For orders that have already been processed or shipped: These cannot be modified, but you can return the items once received according to our return policy.
Shipping

What shipping options are available?

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: Delivery within 3-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: Delivery within 1-3 business days
  • Same-Day Delivery: Available for select locations in Dhaka if ordered before 12 PM

Shipping costs are calculated based on your location and the selected shipping method at checkout.

Shipping

How long will it take to receive my order?

Delivery times depend on your location and the shipping method you choose:

  • Within Dhaka: 1-3 business days
  • Outside Dhaka: 3-7 business days
  • Remote areas: May take up to 10 business days

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number by email to monitor your delivery status.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery if the items are in their original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return:

  1. Contact our customer service at iwantmore.bd999@gmail.com
  2. Include your order number and reason for return
  3. We'll provide return instructions and a return authorization
  4. Once we receive and inspect the returned items, we'll process your refund

Please note that shipping costs are non-refundable, and a restocking fee may apply.

Returns

How do I exchange an item?

To exchange an item, please follow our return process and place a new order for the desired item. This ensures the fastest processing time for your exchange.

If the item you want is at risk of selling out, please contact our customer service team at iwantmore.bd999@gmail.com, and we'll try to accommodate your exchange request directly.

Products

Are your products authentic?

Yes, we guarantee that all products sold on I Want More are 100% authentic. We source our products directly from authorized manufacturers and distributors to ensure quality and authenticity.

If you have any concerns about the authenticity of a product you've received, please contact our customer service team immediately.

Products

How do I know if a product is in stock?

Product availability is indicated on each product page. If an item is in stock, you'll be able to add it to your cart. If an item is out of stock, you may have the option to sign up for a notification when it becomes available again.

For information about restocking timelines, please contact our customer service team.

Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • Mobile Banking (bKash, Nagad, Rocket)
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)
  • Bank Transfer

All online transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption to protect your payment information.

Payment

Is Cash on Delivery (COD) available?

Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for most locations in Bangladesh. You can select COD as your payment method during checkout if it's available for your delivery address.

Please note that for high-value orders, we may require a partial advance payment.
